Pasta

My famous pasta recipe

This is a very basic, but delicious pasta. We will be making the noodles and you can make the sauce if you would like as well, but I will not be covering the sauce recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Parmesan (preferably a block)

Steps:

  1. First you are going to want to clear a space off on a large surface such as a table
  2. There are no amounts when you are traditionally making pasta, if you don't have enough egg or flour you need to add more as you go.
  3. Put a generous well of flour on your table, imagine you are making a bowl out of flour, in the well in the middle add a couple eggs.
  4. Slowly with a fork beat the eggs and mix in flour a little at a time
  5. Then this will stop working and this is where it will get messy, you then need to go ham on it with your hands and just need that sucker
  6. You can optionally add a small amount of olive oil into this as well (maybe a tsp or 2) too much and it will mess up the noodles
  7. When you have a cohesive dough, you will wrap it up and let it rest in the fridge for 30min to and hour if not more (the gluten is very worked and it will be exceedingly difficult to roll out right now)
  8. When it has been rested take it out and roll it into a sheet the thickness of your finger nail.
  9. Chop it into lines around the size of the first joint of your pointer finger, maybe a little more like one and a half of that joint
  10. After you chop one, drag the length of it through some flour on both sides so it doesn't stick.
  11. Take all of your noodles and boil them in salted water. Test as they go so you will know when they are done
  12. Combine the noodles in your sauce pot, add a spoonful of pasta water to the sauce as well.
  13. Grate the block of parmesan on top of your pasta and mix a little in with the sauce as well.
  14. Enjoy!
