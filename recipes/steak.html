Steak

My famous steak recipe

In this recipe we will be making perfectly cooked steaks. In particular I will be making filet mignons

Ingredients:

  • A cut of steak
  • Fresh garlic
  • Fresh thyme
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400-425deg
  2. First you are going to want to pat dry the steak with a paper towel and season with salt and pepper (if using salted butter, don't salt)
  3. I will mark these next steps that can be interchangable with a *. The order is technically supposed to be bake and then sear, but you can do either and it will be delicious.
  4. *Heat up a pan till it is ripping hot and then sear all sides of the steak for 2 minutes, maybe one minute on the small sides
  5. *Bake the steaks for maybe 5-6 minutes.
  6. Heat up butter and minced garlic in a pan with some fresh thyme. Let the garlic cook, but don't let it burn, it will be terrible
  7. When the garlic is cooked begin basting the steaks with the butter thyme garlic mixture
  8. Let the steaks rest for 5-10 minutes
  9. Enjoy!
